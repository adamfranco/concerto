Export Actions

Admin

This action exports the entirety of concerto's data into a compressed archive. The archive will contain all of the metadata in xml files, and all of the raw data (images, videos, audio) in their own files. This archive is organized in such a way that the importer can import the data back into an instantiation of concerto (theoretically in one fell swoop, but also easily in multiple pieces, for subsequent importers).

The archive name should be entered into the text field and the compression chosen from the drop down menu provided. Your browser will prompt you to save the archive or open it; some browsers will give you the option to rename the archive at this point. Choose a location for the browser to send the archive and the download of concerto will commence.

Please be aware that this archive may well be gargantuan, and is being made available as a human readable (XML) backup. Decompressing the archive will take a lot of disk space and should be done only when necessary.

Currently the only available compression is '.tar.gz' and the data that is being exported are the repositories only.
